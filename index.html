Problem Statement

Many learners in rural Kenyan communities lack consistent access to qualified teachers, up-to-date materials, and reliable internet or electricity. This results in lower outcomes and limited opportunities.

Our approach: a light, offline-capable learning app that runs well on low-power, solar-charged devices; syllabus-aligned content; simple assessments; and visual progress tracking for students and teachers.
